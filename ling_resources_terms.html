  • Terms and Definitions

terms and definitions

Conlanging
Stands for "constructed language" - the process of creating your own language, either from an existing source (a posteriori) or from scratch (a priori).
Creole
A blending of more than one language that therefore becomes a language in its own right.
Generalization
When a word expands its definition over time.
Linguistics
The scientific study of language.
Melioration
A word gaining a more positive definition over time.
Morpheme
The smallest unit of a word that has meaning.
Morphology
The study of word formation.
Pejoration
A word gaining a more negative definition over time.
Pidgin
A blending of a primary language and elements of other languages.
Phonetics
The study of sounds.
Phoneme
A unit of sound.
Phonology
The study of sound systems.
Specialization
When the broad definition of a word shrinks over time.
Syntax
The study of sentence structure.
Semantics
The study of meaning.
X-Bar Theory
A method of drawing sentence structure trees.
