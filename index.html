welcome to linguistics 101!

What is Linguistics do you ask? Well you've come to the right place! Linguistics is the scientific study of language. It covers everything from the study of alphabets and writings, to how we conceptualize language in our minds. To start learning about the basics of linguistics, go to the What is Linguistics page.


Linguistics is an incredibly wide field that encompasses many different topics of research. You can explore the different areas of linguistics in the Fields of Linguistics page.


Please, feel free to explore the links and if you have any questions, feel free to contact me!

spotlight - october 2018

Conlanging!

Conlanging is the art of creating a language. Conlanging is short for "constructed language." It is an up and coming field in linguistics and people have been doing it for a lot longer than you think! To read more, check out the Conlanging page!
